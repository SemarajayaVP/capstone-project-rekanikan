Why Choose Rekan Ikan?

Rekan Ikan is a start-up dedicated to ornamental fish lovers in Indonesia. We have a variety of products, the first of which is an automatic fish feeder as a solution to the problem of feeding ornamental fish. In addition, we also have an application to classify ornamental fish species in Indonesia based on photos.

Convenience and Consistency

With the app, users can easily schedule and adjust feeding times without physically being present near the fish tank.

Customizable Feeding Options

Users can tailor the feeding portions, frequencies, and types of food dispensed based on the specific needs of their fish.

Enhanced Knowledge and Care for Fish

the app offers comprehensive care guidelines, namely specific details on optimal water conditions, preferred food types, and other essential care tips tailored to the identified fish.

Aspirasi dan Tujuan Kami

Ikan hias merupakan salah satu hewan yang gemar dipelihara oleh masyarakat luas. Namun, dibalik cantiknya bentuk ikan hias, terdapat berbagai permasalahan yang menyertainya, mulai dari cara perawatan, hingga pemberian makan sehari-hari.

Jumlah Pemelihara Ikan Hias di Indonesia

1,297,021 ornamental fish farmers in Indonesia (BPS 2021) and 7,7 M+ Fish entusiasts (facebook, 2023)

Kesusahan Memberi Makan Secara Teratur

Ornamental fish should be fed 2-3 times a day, but not all fish keepers can do this regularly

Tidak mengerti cara merawat ikan hias

each type of ornamental fish has a different way of care, therefore, a solution is needed how to easily find out how to care for ornamental fish based only on the photo.
